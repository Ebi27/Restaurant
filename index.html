Our Restaurant

We've never thought of our restaurants as temples of gastronomy, they're just places where the fish is fresh and the atmosphere alive and full.

Philosophy

Our philosophy and goal is to provide guests with a one-of-a-kind experience by combining delicious food, unique interior design, a great music selection, and friendly service.

Passion for food

Seafood Bar invites you to try our specially composed menu where our Head Chef put the twist on classics and created dishes that bring pleasure to the senses, using high quality ingredients, local products and innovative combinations.

Interior

Seafood Bar is housed in a historic building that dates back to the thirteenth century and was designed in the spirit of the fin-de-siècle. We have made every effort to ensure that the historic elements of our restaurant's interiors are preserved, such as the remains of window frames, hinges, grottoes, and the oldest, original iron window grille in the bar hall, which is the only such monument

Passion

We want to infect our guests with the same enthusiasm for culinary art that we have for it. Our Chef explores new surprising flavors and gives classic dishes a contemporary character by combining modernity and tradition. We use the full potential of the region, bringing our guests closer to the beauty and fertility of Malopolska, by having the best products from local suppliers at our disposal.
