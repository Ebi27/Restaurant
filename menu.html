Our Menu

Seafood Bar serves the highest quality seafood, fish, oysters and meats served in combination with seasonal, regional products.

With an interior inspired by iconic old Markets and Fish Halls from around the world, at Seafood Station you can eat, among others, Lobsters, Crabs, classic Oysters Rockefeller, perfectly grilled tuna steak or the beloved, authentic British Fish & Chips. Combined with a carefully selected menu of fine wines, beers and cocktails, we offer unforgettable culinary memories!
